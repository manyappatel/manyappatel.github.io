I'm a creative strategist drawn to the intersection of culture, commerce, and storytelling — specifically how brands in fashion, retail technology, and luxury earn genuine attention and loyalty.

My hands-on experience founding and running Pearlescent, a jewelry and apparel brand, gave me an operator's perspective: I've built brand identities from scratch, managed end-to-end campaigns, and learned what it actually takes to grow a product people care about.

Since then, I've built my skills across multiple fashion-tech internships focusing on growth, marketing, and AI integration.

I'm energized by brand problems that require both analytical rigor and visual intuition — whether that's repositioning a heritage label, mapping an AI trend landscape, or crafting the story that makes a product feel inevitable.
